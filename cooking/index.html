A record of the meals my wife Kate and I have cooked together for Friday Night Dinner.

BBQ Winter Ribs + Big Herby Potato Wedges

Very simple to make, but requires patience. Ribs need 2 hours at 120c first, and than an hour of 10 min intervals at 160c adding layers of BBQ sauce every 10 mins.
Meat was gorgeous. Sauce was a little bland (we used Heinz). Would like to use a thinner sauce/some way for the sauce to get deeper into the meat.

Creamy Chicken & Chorizo Pasta w/ Garlic Doughknots

Pasta dish was quite similar to Kate's Cajun Chicken Pasta, but a thinner consistency. Made with double cream and made thicker with helpings of grated parmesan.

Homemade McDonald's - Big Mac for Tom, Plain Double Cheeseburger for Kate; Chicken Nuggets; Large Fries

Unbelieveable how similar everything was, from the Big Mac sauce to the size and texture of the fries. Very time consuming. Definitely a one-off. Chicken Nuggets were made too big, and we didn't grind the chicken mince enough, so they had a meaty texture which was the only missed note.
Surprised how easy it was to create smashed burgers, and will be making those as a quick dinner in the future!

Beef & Veg Stew, Garlic Herb Mashed Potatoes, and Roasted Brussel Sprouts; Homemade Brownie & Ice Cream

Chicken Carbonara, Homemade Garlic Bread, and Grilled Broccoli
